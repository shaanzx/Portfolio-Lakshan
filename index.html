IJSE KOUL WASANATHAYA

The IJSE Koul Wasanthya captures the vibrant spirit of the traditional Awurudu Uthsawaya (New Year festival) celebrated at our institute. This joyful event brought students and faculty together in a celebration filled with cultural games, traditional attire, and time-honored customs. From the thrill of tug-of-war and sack races to the charm of crowning the Awurudu Kumara and Kumari, every moment was a beautiful blend of heritage and fun. Browse through the gallery to experience the color, energy, and joy that made Koul Wasanthya unforgettable.
